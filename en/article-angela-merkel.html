Angela Merkel 16 years in power

How is Angela Merkel ?

Angela Merkel was born on July 17, 1954, in Hamburg in Germany. His real last name is Kanser, the Merkel name came from her first husband Ulrich Merkel. She married him in 1977 and stayed with him for 5 years therefore divorced in 1982 without having children. She decides to Keep Merkel’s name. Then she will marry again in 1998 to Joachim Sauer with whom she will not have any children either having children.

Angela Merkel is a German politician and she is also the leader of the Christian Democratic Union Union from 2000 to 2018. she also occupies a very important (role) in the German state which she has been the Federal Chancellor of Germanysince November 22, 2005 to 8 December 2021. So, a total of 15 years.

She was elected three times in a row by the German parliament. Before reaching this position, she was Federal Minister for women and youth from 1991 to 1994. Before being entrusted with the Federal Ministry for the Environment, Nature Conservation and Nuclear Safety for the next 4 years, from 1994 to 1998. For the next two years she did not hold such an important position. But in 2000 she became the first woman in the CDU (Christian Democratic Union of Germany). In 2005 she won the elections and became the first female chancellor in German history. And during her tenure as the chancellor, Merkel has been frequently referred as the de facto leader of European union and also as the most powerful woman in the world.

Chancellor Angela Merkel

Her beginning as a chancellor was not well regarded by everyone according to the German press.

Angela Merkel was under pressure because of the refusal of Gerhard Schröder (Federal Chancellor of Germany. Between 1998 and 2005). For her candidacy in the elections as Chancellor. In the Bundestag (German federal parliament) the SPD party has 4 more seats than Angela Merkel’s party. And can easily refuse the passage of the latter to the post of chancellor.

After 5 weeks of negotiations. The SPD and the CDU united with the CSU reach an agreement in which Angela Merkel can participate in the federal elections in Germany (Bundeskanzlerin). She was elected by the Bundestag on 22 November 2005 with 397 votes out of 611 (64.9%).

Angela Merkel becomes at the age of 51 the youngest political figure to reach the position of chancellor. And the first woman to reach this position. In this period Angela Merkel has shown that she is worthy of this position. She has brought a lot to Germany and has faced the financial crisis in 2008 and the crisis of the COVID 19.

At the End of her first term, her record was very good. She was elected 4 times in a row. And this is her last time because she accepted to welcome Syrian refugees. But her opponents did not appreciate this action. So, they began to hold manifestations. To reduce the tensions, Angela Merkel has announced that this fourth mandate will be her last.

A figure of feminism

Angela Merkel is considered an emblematic figure of feminism. For what she has show to the world that also a women succeed in politics and in the parliament of Europa she is referred as the most powerful woman in the world. and one of his most famous Quotes about Women is It is more and more normal that women reach the highest responsibilities.
